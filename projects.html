My Story

Builder. Designer. Problem Solver.

I'm Lubna Janaan Jiffry, a Software Engineering graduate currently completing my BEng (Hons) at APIIT Sri Lanka / Staffordshire University. I build things at the intersection of clean engineering and thoughtful design.

My work spans full-stack web applications, AI/ML tools, browser games, and design-focused frontends. Whether it's training a YOLOv8 model on custom datasets or crafting a pixel-perfect luxury UI, I bring the same level of care and intention.

Before diving into software engineering, I spent time as a lecturer at Aspire Business School — teaching Economics and Business Studies to A-Level students. That experience sharpened my communication, planning, and mentoring skills in ways that still show up in how I approach collaborative projects.

I also worked as a Marketing Coordinator at RJ Dezign Studio, managing social media strategy, campaign analytics, and client communications. This gave me a rare perspective — understanding both the technical side and how products need to connect with real people.

Currently based in Western Province, Kohuwala, Sri Lanka and open to junior developer roles, internships, and freelance projects — remote or on-site.
